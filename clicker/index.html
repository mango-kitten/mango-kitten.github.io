Tutorials

Yarn

You get yarn by clicking the ball of yarn on the HOME page.

Yarn per Click

Yarn per Click, or ypc, is the amount of yarn earned each time you click the yarn ball. It can be increased by buying upgrades or adopting cats.

Cats

Once you get enough money, you can go to the ADOPT page. Clicking 'find a stray' will get a random cat from the strays, and clicking adopt will keep it.
Different cats give different bonuses, and you may have one cat from each set at a time.

Catalogue

Found on the CATALOGUE page, this handy tool automatically tracks how many of each type of cat you have gotten.
Later in development, hitting a certain threshold of cats will give a bonus.

CPS

10 times a second, you gain an amount of yarn equal to (cps/10)*(ypc*prestigebonus). 1 CPS equates to 1 click on the yarn ball every second.

Prestige

Once you get a cat from the fruit bowl, you unlock the PRESTIGE tab. Prestiging resets your current cats and yarn, but keeps all catalogue progress. Prestiging will give upgrades to yarn earnings, as is shown at the bottom of the page.

Set Boosts

BOOSTING a cat set multiplies all the bonuses from cats a part of that set by 2.
ELEVATING a cat set multiplies all the bonuses from cats a part of that set by 9.
ASCENDING a cat set multiplies the bonuses by 81.
INSPIRING a cat set multiplies the bonuses by 729.

Buy until Better

Clicking this button, found on the adopt screen, buys until you get a better cat, run out of money, or reach the limit granted by your prestige level.
Prestige level 4 - Buy until Better x10
Prestige level 5000 - Buy until Better x100

Increasing cat rolls

Found at some prestige levels, this directly increases the rolls you get when adopting cats, improving your chances of getting good cats.
